Privacy Policy

App: Sixth Man Trivia

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Sixth Man Trivia ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Sixth Man Trivia, we may collect:

  • Account information such as username, email address, and user ID
  • Gameplay data such as quiz scores, progress, streaks, XP, and rewards
  • Public profile information such as username and leaderboard score
  • Basic device and app usage information used for performance and stability

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide app features and save progress
  • Upload scores and game results

3. Leaderboards And Public Data

Sixth Man Trivia includes leaderboard features. Your username and scores may be displayed publicly inside the app. Sensitive personal information, such as your email address, is not displayed publicly on leaderboards.

4. User Consent

By using gameplay and leaderboard features, you acknowledge that scores and related public gameplay information may be uploaded and shown inside the app experience.

5. Storage And Security

We use cloud services, including Google Firebase, to help store app data. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information from unauthorized access.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase

  • Analytics and crash reporting tools
  • AdMob for advertising

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information when necessary to:

  • Operate app functionality
  • Support leaderboard and account systems
  • Comply with legal obligations

8. Data Retention

We keep user data while accounts remain active or as needed to provide the app’s services. You may request account deletion by contacting us.

9. Children’s Privacy

Sixth Man Trivia is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

You may request to:
